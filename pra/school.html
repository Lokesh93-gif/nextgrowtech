School Life

The oldest school in Delhi is the Anglo-Arabic Senior Secondary School, located near Ajmeri Gate, established in 1696 by Ghaziuddin Khan, a general under Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb, initially as Madrasa Ghaziuddin, making it over 300 years old and the oldest surviving school in North India.
